About Me

Hi, my name is Ryan Milton. I have been in the US Navy for 4 years but most recently for about the past 6 months I have started my career as a software developer.
I have been a part of multiple teams developing many applications but one that has had the most impact was developing a web application for West Coast Entertainment to help streamline their event planning process by automatically compiling a list of needed items for an event based on inputs made by an employee. This really helped them boost efficiency because prior to the development of this application an employee had to write out by hand which items went with which events.
I am currently looking for a position that would allow me to create new software and applications as a part of a team or on my own. I would like to use my experience with following strict guidelines, while still implementing my own creativity, to produce software that is competitive in the market and would benefit your organization.
